AP Item Changes

Patch 5.13 brought on a profusion of changes to AP items. The changes to AP items that occurred in this patch for the Summoner's Rift map are as follows:

Blasting Wand

Cost: 860 gold -> 850 gold

Needlessly Large Rod

Cost: 1600 gold -> 1250 gold
Ability Power: 80 -> 60

Rabadon's Deathcap

Cost: 3300 gold -> 3500 gold
Recipe: Needlessly Large Rod / Blasting Wand / 840 gold -> Needlessly Large Rod + Blasting Wand + Amplifying Tome + 935 gold
Ability Power Bonus: 30% -> 35%

Zhonya's Hourglass

Cost: 3300 gold -> 3000 gold
Ability Power: 120 -> 100

Luden's Echo

Cost: 3300 gold -> 3000 gold
Ability Power: 120 -> 100
Movement speed: 7% - 10%

Rylai's Crystal Scepter

Cost: 2900 gold -> 3000 gold
Ability Power: 100 -> 100
Health: 400 -> 400
Recipe: Giant's Belt / Blasting Wand / Amplifying Tome / 605 gold -> Giant's Belt + Needlessly Large Rod + Amplifying Tome + 315 Gold
Single Target Slow: 35% -> 40%
Area of Effect Slow (Instant): 15% -> 40%
Damage Over Time or Multi-hit Slow: 15% for 1.5 seconds -> 20% for 1 second
NEW I Choose You: Summoned minions (e.g. Mordekaiser's R - Children of the Grave, Annie's R - Summon: Tibbers) now slow on-hit 20% for 1 second
NEW Crystal Clear: New particle added

Archangel's Staff

Cost: 2700 gold -> 3000 gold
Recipe: Tear of the Goddess / Blasting Wand / 1120 gold -> Tear of the Goddess + Needlessly Large Rod + 1030 gold
Ability Power: 60 -> 80

Seraph's Embrace

Ability Power: 60 -> 80

Rod of Ages

Cost: 2800 gold -> 2700 gold
Base Health: 450 -> 300
Base Mana: 450 -> 400
Health Growth: Unchanged
Mana Growth: 20 per minute (200 mana after 10 minutes) -> 40 per minute (400 mana after 10 minutes)
Ability Power Growth: 2 per minute (20 ability power after 10 minutes) -> 4 per minute (40 ability power after 10 minutes)

Haunting Guise

Cost: 1480 gold -> 1500 gold

Liandry's Torment

Cost: 2900 gold -> 3000 gold
Recipe: Haunting guise / Amplifying Tome / 980 gold -> Haunting Guise + Blasting Wand + 650 gold
Ability Power: 50 -> 80

Void Staff

Cost: 2295 gold -> 2500 gold
Ability Power: 70 -> 80

Nashor's Tooth

Cost: 2920 gold -> 3000 gold
Attack Speed: 50% -> 40%
Ability Power: 60 -> 80

Will of the Ancients

Spell Vamp: 20% -> 0%
NEW True Blood: Your abilities heal you for 15% of the damage dealt, calculated before your opponent's resistances (33% effect for AoE)

Morellonomicon

Cost: Unchanged
Recipe: Fiendish Codex / Forbidden Idol / 880 gold -> Fiendish Codex + Forbidden Idol + Amplifying Tome + 445 gold

Athene's Unholy Grail

Cost: Unchanged
Recipe: Fiendish Codex / Chalice of Harmony / 980 gold -> Fiendish Codex + Chalice of Harmony + Amplifying Tome + 545 gold
